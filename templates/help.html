Help & Support

Welcome to the SmartDataHub Help Center. Here you'll find resources to help you get the most out of our platform.

Getting Started

SmartDataHub helps you analyze and visualize your data with powerful AI capabilities. Here's how to get started:

  • Upload your data files (CSV, Excel, JSON, TXT) through the dashboard
  • Our AI will automatically analyze the data and suggest visualizations
  • Select charts to view detailed visualizations of your data
  • Save charts that you want to reference later
  • Generate comprehensive reports of your findings
  • Chat with our AI assistant to gain deeper insights

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?
SmartDataHub supports CSV, Excel (XLSX, XLS), JSON, and TXT file formats. We recommend structured data formats like CSV and Excel for the best results.
How does the AI analysis work?
Our AI analyzes your data structure, content, and statistical properties to identify patterns, anomalies, and insights. It then suggests appropriate visualizations and generates natural language explanations of key findings.
Can I export my charts and reports?
Yes, you can export reports in both PDF and Markdown formats. Reports can include any selection of your saved charts along with AI-generated insights about your data.
Is my data secure?
Yes, SmartDataHub takes data security seriously. Your data is encrypted during transmission and storage. We do not share your data with third parties, and you can delete your data at any time.
What if I need help with specific analysis?
You can chat with our AI assistant directly on the dashboard. For more complex needs, contact our support team via email.
